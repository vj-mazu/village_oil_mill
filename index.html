Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Made with Love

Established in 2024, Village Oil Mill was born from a desire to bring back the purity of traditional wood-pressed oils to modern kitchens.

Located at the Dept of Processing and Food Engineering, CAE, UAS, Raichur, we combine age-old methods with scientific standards to ensure you get nothing but the best.
